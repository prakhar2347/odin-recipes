Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, all baked to perfection. Each layer adds depth and flavor, making it a hearty and comforting meal. Its versatility allows for endless variations, from traditional Bolognese to vegetarian or even seafood versions, making lasagna a beloved favorite around the world.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Beef
  • Cheese
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the beef and drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with cheese on top.
  9. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  11. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
